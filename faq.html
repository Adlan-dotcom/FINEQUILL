Frequently Asked Questions

Browse by Category

To place an order:

  1. Browse our products and add items to your cart
  2. Click the shopping cart icon to review your order
  3. Proceed to checkout
  4. Enter your shipping and payment information
  5. Review your order and click "Place Order"

You'll receive an order confirmation email once your order is successfully placed.

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Online Banking (FPX)
  • GrabPay
  • Touch 'n Go eWallet
  • ShopeePay

Shipping times depend on your location and the shipping method selected:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days (Peninsular Malaysia)
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days (Peninsular Malaysia)
  • East Malaysia: 5-7 business days

Please note that processing time (1-2 business days) is additional to shipping time.

Yes! We offer free standard shipping for all orders over RM100 within Peninsular Malaysia. For orders below RM100, shipping fees apply.

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for most unused and unopened items in their original packaging. Some items like ink cartridges cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service at returns@finequill.com with your order number and reason for return.

For full details, please see our Returns & Refunds Policy.

Many of our pens are refillable! Product descriptions will indicate if a pen is refillable and what type of refill it uses. We also sell refills separately for most models.

Our notebook paper weights vary by product:

  • Standard notebooks: 70-80gsm
  • Premium notebooks: 90-100gsm
  • Sketchbooks: 120-150gsm

The specific paper weight is listed in each product's description.
