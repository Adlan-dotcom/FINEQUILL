Returns & Refunds Policy

Return Policy

We accept returns within 14 days of receipt for most unused and unopened items in their original packaging. To be eligible for a return:

  • The item must be unused and in the same condition you received it
  • The item must be in its original packaging
  • You must provide proof of purchase

Certain items cannot be returned for hygiene reasons (e.g., ink cartridges, correction tape).

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact our customer service at returns@finequill.com to request a Return Authorization
  2. Package the item securely with all original packaging and accessories
  3. Include the Return Authorization number on the package
  4. Ship the item back to us using a trackable method

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved:

  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  • You will be responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect

Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange an item, please contact us at service@finequill.com.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 3 days of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund and cover return shipping costs.
