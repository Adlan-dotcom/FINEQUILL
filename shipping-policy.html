Shipping Policy

Delivery Options

We offer several shipping methods to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days (RM5)
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days (RM10)
  • Free Shipping: For orders over RM100 (Standard Shipping only)

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and public holidays). Orders placed after 2pm will be processed the next business day.

Delivery Areas

We currently ship to all states in Peninsular Malaysia. For East Malaysia (Sabah & Sarawak), please contact us for shipping rates.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package using our Order Tracking page.

International Shipping

We currently only ship within Malaysia. For international inquiries, please contact us.

Undeliverable Packages

If a package is returned to us as undeliverable, we will contact you to arrange reshipment. Additional shipping fees may apply.
