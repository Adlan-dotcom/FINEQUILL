Who We Are

FineQuill was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality stationery that enhances the writing experience. What started as a small shop in Kuala Lumpur has grown into a beloved brand across Malaysia, known for our carefully curated selection of writing instruments, notebooks, and office essentials.

We believe that the tools you use matter. Whether you're a student taking notes, a professional signing important documents, or someone who simply enjoys the tactile pleasure of writing, FineQuill products are designed to elevate your experience.
